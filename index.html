London Bus Pulse

An interactive day on six central TfL bus corridors

TfL BUSTO 2025-2026 weekday demand · route geometry and timetables from TfL Unified API

This project follows the structure of a transit story: vehicles, passengers, crowding, and a route you choose. The map uses real TfL bus geometries; passenger demand comes from BUSTO boardings, alightings, and vehicle load.

The Buses

Six selected routes thread through central London. Drag the Marey diagram to move the day: direction-separated dots show scheduled buses moving along real road geometry.

Source
